7 quick ways to monetize a new site

Aug 16, 2025 · 4 min read

  1. Affiliate links: Add them to genuine recommendations. Use rel="sponsored nofollow".
  2. Payment links: Sell a simple checklist or template using Stripe Payment Links.
  3. Services: Offer a small consulting package in your niche.
  4. Newsletter sponsors: Add a short sponsor blurb in a weekly email.
  5. Info products: A 5-page guide that solves a specific problem.
  6. Job board: Curate 10 jobs/week in your niche with paid listings.
  7. Ads: Once traffic grows, enable display ads.
