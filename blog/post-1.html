How to pick a niche and validate demand

Aug 16, 2025 · 5 min read

Picking the right niche is the highest-leverage decision for a new website. Great niches are specific, have a clear audience, and contain real problems worth solving.

1) Start with pains, not passions

List recurring problems people complain about in communities you know. Problems attract traffic; solutions create products.

2) Check search demand

Use free tools to estimate monthly searches. Look for clusters of related keywords with clear intent.

3) Evaluate monetization paths

Identify at least two revenue options (affiliate, products, services, ads) for the same audience.

4) Run a quick validation

Publish one high-quality guide and share it in relevant communities. Watch for engagement and questions.
